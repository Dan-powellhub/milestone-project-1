Who We Are

Welcome to Community Gym: Your Local Community Gym

At Community Gym, we're more than just a gym - we're a space where the community comes together to stay active, healthy, and connected. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or just starting your wellness journey, our gym offers something for everyone in a welcoming, supportive environment.

What You Can Do Here:

Strength & Cardio Training:
Our fully equipped fitness suite includes free weights, resistance machines, treadmills, bikes, and more.

Group Classes:
From Pump and Hyrox to high-intensity interval training (HIIT), circuits, and dancercise, our diverse class schedule has something to suit every taste and fitness level.

Personal Training:
Work one-on-one with our qualified trainers to set goals, build confidence, and get results.

Open Gym Access:
Drop in anytime during our open hours to use the facilities at your own pace.

Community Events & Challenges:
Stay motivated with regular wellness challenges, fitness competitions, and social events designed to keep the community spirit strong.

Our Purpose:

Our mission is to promote health and wellbeing for all ages and abilities. We believe fitness should be inclusive, affordable, and accessible. That's why we aim to create a space where everyone feels welcome; no matter your experience or fitness level.

We're here to help you move more, feel better, and live a healthier life, all while connecting with others in your community.

Come in, say hello, and see what makes Community Gym a great place to get active and stay connected.
