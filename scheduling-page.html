Pump

Every Thursday at 2pm

A "pump class," often referred to as a BODYPUMP class or "Power Pump," is a high-intensity,
resistance-based fitness class that utilizes a barbell with light to moderate weights and a high number of repetitions
to work all major muscle groups. These classes are designed to be fun,
fast-paced, and effective for building lean muscle, improving endurance, and burning calories.

Hiit

Every Monday at 11am

HIIT stands for high-intensity interval training.
It's a way of training that combines quick, intense bursts of exercise,
where you're working out as hard as you can, with short periods of recovery.

Hyrox

Every Friday at 9am

A Hyrox class is a group fitness training session that prepares
individuals for the Hyrox fitness race, a global competition combining running and functional exercises.
It's designed to be challenging and accessible to all fitness levels,
focusing on building strength, endurance, and functional movement skills.

>

Bootcamp

Every Tuesday at 11am

Bootcamp gym classes are high-intensity, group-based workouts
that blend strength training, cardio, and calisthenics, often using interval training.
They are designed to be challenging and foster a sense of community, with instructors often adopting a motivating,
sometimes sergeant-like, approach. Expect a mix of exercises like
push-ups, squats, lunges, and sprints, potentially using weights or resistance bands.

Dancercise

Every Saturday at 1pm

Dancercise is a form of exercise that combines dancing with fitness,
using choreographed dance routines to improve fitness levels.
It's a fun and energetic way to get a workout,
often done in classes with instructors guiding participants through various dance moves and styles.

Circuits

Every Wednesday at 6pm

Typically, a circuit training class will consist of a number of different exercise stations being set up around a room.
The participants will be split up into groups of two or three people
each starting at one specific station and doing the exercise at that station for a set period of time.
